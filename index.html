关于我 About Me

我目前是香港大学地理系的博士后研究员，在宫鹏教授团队工作。我的研究聚焦于智慧建模与制图，致力于将辐射传输理论应用于大尺度遥感监测。

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Geography at The University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Peng Gong's team. My research focuses on intelligent modeling and mapping, aiming to apply radiative transfer theory to large-scale remote sensing monitoring.
